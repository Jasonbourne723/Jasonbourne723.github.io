## there was/ were

1. I was hungry,but when I got home,there was nothing to eat.
2. there was a train at 10.30 o'clock
3. there was a party last night

## there has/have been

1. last week I went back the town where I was born , there have been a lot of changes.
2. look, there has been an accident, it is so dangerous here, there have been many accidents.

## there will be

1. I am going away, there are packing to do today, because there won't be time tomorrow
2. do you think there will be a lot of people at the party on Saturday?
3. the manager of the company is leaving , so there will be a new manager soon.
