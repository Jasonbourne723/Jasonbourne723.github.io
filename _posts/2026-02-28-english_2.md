## be doing/do 表将来

- be doing 表示将来计划要做的事
- do 表示将来已经确定的事
- what are you going to do on evening ?
- what are you doing on Saturday evening?
- what time does the train leave on evening?
- what time are you leaving tomorrow?
- we are having a party next Saturday,would you like to come?

## I'm going to do 

我已决定要做...(但可能还没有安排去做)
be doing 一般用于表示已安排进行的动作

- he is going to fall into the hole
- there are a lot of clouds in the sky. It's going to rain.

- I feel a bit hungry,I think I'll eat something
- I don't think I'll go out tonight,I am so tired.
- I can see you're busy,I won't stay long.
- the weather doesn't look very good,Do you think it'll rain?
- sally and David are in love,Do you think they'll get married?
